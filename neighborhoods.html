Featured Neighborhoods

Canton

Canton is located in South East Baltimore and has so much to offer. The types of homes offered here include traditional rowhomes, luxury condo buildings, and a few recently constructed rowhome communities. This historic neighborhood is full of old charm while also offering modern shopping centers. The neighborhood is walkable to charming corner shops and restaurants and there is no lack of nightlife. Canton residents enjoy two public parks in the area and the waterfront.

Fells Point

Fells point in Southeast Baltimore is a waterfront community that is full of fun. Lined with cobblestone streets, residents enjoy the walkability of the area to the many neighborhood shops and restaurants.The historic, charming rowhomes are abundant, including the oldest standing residence in Baltimore City.

Fed Hill

Located in South Baltimore, Federal Hill is full of history. Residents enjoy the Federal Hill Park located in the area, which the neighborhood was named after. Housing types include traditional rowhomes, as well as a plethora of luxury condo buildings. Nightlife is an attraction in the area, as well as the walkability and fun environment.

Hampden

Hampden is a lively neighborhood in Northern Baltimore that is full of character and art. Residents enjoy “The Avenue” which is the main street in the neighborhood that offers a multitude of shops and restaurants. The Miracle on 34th Street is a main attraction in the area that boasts stunning, cheerful lights each holiday season.

Greektown

Greektown, located in East Baltimore, is full of fantastic restaurants. These hidden gems are plentiful when residents are looking for a bite to eat. The homes offered here include traditional rowhomes and newer construction townhome neighborhoods. The neighborhood hosts the annual Greek Festival that is enjoyed by many.
